Conditional Prompt Tuning for Multimodal Fusion

A parameter-efficient prompt-tuning approach for multimodal fusion that reaches fine-tuning-level performance while updating only a small fraction of the model parameters.

CLIP-Count: Towards Text-Guided Zero-Shot Object Counting

An end-to-end object counting framework that uses text prompts to count arbitrary categories without category-specific supervision.

AvatarCraft: Transforming Text into Neural Human Avatars with Parameterized Shape and Pose Control

A full pipeline for creating, editing, and animating neural human avatars directly from text while keeping explicit control over body shape and pose.

Nerf-Art: Text-Driven Neural Radiance Fields Stylization

A text-guided stylization method for neural radiance fields that turns implicit 3D scenes into coherent artistic renderings with a target visual style.

Learning a Neural Radiance Field for Stylized Scene

Early exploration into stylized scene generation with neural radiance fields, focused on bridging expressive artistic control and 3D scene representation.

Realtime Hatching Rendering

A non-photorealistic rendering study centered on interactive hatching effects, emphasizing visual rhythm, legibility, and painterly structure in real-time graphics.
